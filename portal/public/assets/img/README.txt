8 West IT RMM — logo assets
===========================

Drop your logo files here with these EXACT names. The portal uses them
automatically; until they exist it falls back to a plain "8" badge + text.

  logo-horizontal.png   <- the wide "8 West IT, LLC" logo with WHITE text
                           (transparent background). Shown in the top nav bar.
                           Recommended height ~80px (any width).

  logo-stacked.png      <- the square/stacked shield logo (the one on the
                           navy rounded badge works great). Shown on the
                           login + setup screens. Recommended ~400x400px.

  favicon.ico           <- browser tab icon (square shield). 32x32 or 48x48.
  apple-touch-icon.png  <- home-screen icon for phones. 180x180px.

Tip: you can make favicon.ico / apple-touch-icon.png from the square shield
logo at https://realfavicongenerator.net (free).
